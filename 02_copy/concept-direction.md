# Sirius Beauty & Wellness Med Spa · Concept Direction (for Apollo)

**Trade:** Medical aesthetics / med spa · **City:** Lakewood, CO · **Register:** Luxury
**Written by:** Dharma · **Grounded in:** verified facts from the live site (services, provider, RN supervision, bilingual care, financing, hours, contact)

## The central angle
**Couture aesthetics. One expert hand.**
Advanced medical beauty, measured to the individual, delivered by a single provider who never hands you off, and watched over by a registered nurse. The luxury is not marble and gold. The luxury is the attention.

## Why this angle
The under-used fact is the strongest sale: at Sirius, *one* master provider (Marisela Infante) performs every treatment herself, start to finish, with RN medical supervision behind her. Most med spas are either assembly-line clinical or spa-fluffy. Sirius is bespoke medical artistry. That contrast is the whole position.

## The fear to defuse
For this client, the fear is intimidation: being judged, being upsold, being processed. Luxury defuses it through discretion, unhurried time, and being met where you are. Never through pressure.
