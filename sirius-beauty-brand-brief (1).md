# SIRIUS BEAUTY & WELLNESS MED SPA
## Brand Brief — meaning, exact system, constraints, amplification

---

### 1. THE MEANING

Sirius is the brightest star in the night sky. That is the whole brand, and right now it is being wasted.

The meaning: a **guiding light for your beauty, in your language**. Sirius is the bilingual, Spanish-first med spa where Latina clients are understood completely, treated by a licensed esthetician and laser tech who speaks to them like family and delivers real, natural results. Not a discount counter. The brightest, most trusted point of light in a crowded sky.

This reframes everything. The sparkle in the current feed is not cheap glitter, it is **starlight**, and it should be used on purpose, with discipline. Amethyst night sky, silver starlight, one bright guiding star.

**What it stands for:** results you can see, spoken in your language, from someone you trust.

**One line (bilingual by design):** *Tu belleza, tu idioma, tu estrella.* Your beauty, your language, your star.

---

### 2. THE EXACT SYSTEM  (definition)

The look is a luminous amethyst night sky lit by silver starlight. Deep, rich, calm, premium. One system, used everywhere, no exceptions.

**Color** *(sampled from the brand's own graphics; confirm against vector master)*

| Role | Name | Hex | Exact use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Signature dark | Deep Amethyst | `#3D3352` | primary ground, hero backgrounds |
| Accent | Royal Violet | `#6E4E9E` | buttons, highlights, one focal element *(estimated, verify)* |
| Light ground | Soft Lavender | `#E4DCF0` | airy backgrounds, editorial space |
| Starlight | Pewter Silver | `#B9BBC2` | the wordmark, fine lines, star accents |
| Contrast | Ink | `#141216` | photography grounds, high-drama frames |

**Exact contrast rules (non-negotiable, this is where the feed currently fails):**
- Body text: Ink or white on Soft Lavender, or white on Deep Amethyst. Both clear 4.5:1.
- Pewter Silver is metallic decoration only. Never body text on light. It fails contrast and disappears.
- Royal Violet text only at 18px bold or larger.
- Minimum body size 16px. Promo cards currently cram sub-12px legal lines that no one can read.

**Type**
- Display: a high-contrast serif with a subtle astral feel for headlines and the wordmark lockup.
- Body: one clean sans, generous spacing, bilingual-ready (must carry Spanish accents and ñ cleanly).
- Script: one warm script for a single human phrase per asset, never for price or claims.
- Two voices per asset maximum. The current mix of four fonts per promo is the problem.

**Texture and light**
Amethyst gradients, soft starlight glints, real skin in warm light. Sparkle is fine when it reads as *night sky*, never as confetti. Matte over garish. Space over clutter.

---

### 3. CONSTRAINTS, GUARDRAILS, AND AMPLIFICATION

**Senior constraints (the discipline the brand lacks today)**
- **One visual system.** Retire the neon-green "Spooky" language and the black-glitter "Black Friday" language entirely. They belong to no brand. Everything lives in the amethyst-and-starlight system.
- **Promo discipline.** No more than one active promotion visible at a time, and no more than one promo in any four posts. Promos use the same premium system as everything else, never a louder, cheaper one.
- **Price is not the message.** Lead with the result, the expertise, and the transformation. Price is a detail inside the offer, not the headline.
- **Consistent identity.** One handle casing, one spa name lockup, one phone treatment across every surface.

**Amplification (the actual growth lever)**
Sirius will not grow by discounting harder. It grows by amplifying the two assets competitors cannot copy:
1. **The bilingual authority.** Own Spanish-first med-spa content in metro Denver. Educational, warm, in-language. This is the moat. Nobody else can credibly hold it.
2. **The owner and the transformation.** Marisela on camera plus real, consented before/afters, framed as stories, not price tags. Authority content converts at full price. Discounts convert once and erode the brand.
Shift the content rhythm to: education and authority most of the time, transformation stories often, promotions rarely and premium. That single change moves Sirius from lowest-price to most-trusted, which is where the margin lives.

**GUARDRAILS (compliance, non-negotiable)**
- Body-contouring and fat-reduction claims (Lipoflack, Endolaser) and weight-loss (semaglutide) are heavily regulated. No guaranteed, permanent, or specific-loss promises. Semaglutide advertising is a current FDA/FTC hotspot, handle with counsel.
- Every before/after needs written consent, consistent conditions, and a "results vary" note, in both languages.
- No "natural, no pain, no downtime" as an absolute on injectable or laser procedures. Qualify it.
- Verify all of the above with healthcare-marketing counsel.
